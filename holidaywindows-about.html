Our story

Built by a parent
who got tired of winging it.

Holiday Windows started as a spreadsheet. A slightly obsessive spreadsheet, built at 11pm after realising the July school holidays were six weeks away and nothing was booked.

Every year, the same problem. School holidays arrive, four times a year, every year, completely predictable, and somehow they still feel like a surprise. Not the holidays themselves, but everything that needs to happen before them. The leave applications, the holiday care bookings, the flight searches, the realisation that Bali in July costs twice as much if you start looking in June.

"I knew exactly how many days of annual leave I had. I had no idea how many days of school holidays I needed to cover."

That's the gap Holiday Windows was built to close. Not a travel agency. Not a generic deals newsletter. A tool that starts from the actual problem, every school holiday day needs covering, and you have a finite number of leave days to work with, and helps families figure out the optimal mix of annual leave, public holidays, and holiday care across all four windows of the year.

The deals came later. Once you know which windows you're targeting and how many leave days each costs, finding genuinely good deals becomes the last piece. We send one email every Tuesday, no noise, no filler. If there's nothing worth booking that week, we say so.

Holiday Windows is free to use and free to subscribe to. We earn small affiliate commissions from providers when you book through our links. That's the whole business model, and we're upfront about it in every email.
